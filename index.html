Scheduler app

I was part of the Organizing Committee of an AIESEC Conference that hosted more than 400 people for 5 days and 4 nights. I developed a full stack web application for the occassion using PosgreSQL, Apache and Django on a Fedora server. It could:

  • Generate unique badges for all event attendants with a QR code that could be used for quick registration
  • Let attendants see their room number, bus number, and other important information
  • Dynamically create a customized schedule for every attendant based on their department and the sessions they signed up for through the app
  • Let people see public contact information of all other attendants.
  • Detect whether an user is part of the staff, and allow them to see aditional information and take privileged actions within the regular application views

Source code

SmartBoard

During my time as an Executive Board member in AIESEC Andes I developed an organizational scoreboard to know the current state of our operations in our four (later six) core products. It loads the data daily, directly from the operational systems (EXPA) and lets users see their rankings at a national, regional and international level, monthly and yearly goal fulfillment, the people who have taken one of our products in a given month, monthly performance graphs, among others.
